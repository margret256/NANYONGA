MY PORTFOLIO

Margret Nanyonga CEO & Founder |Ora Niam Interiors





As the founder and CEO of Ora Niam Interiors, I bring over 38 years of passion, precision,and innovation to interior design. What started as a dream to transform ordinary rooms into inspiring environments has grown
into a trusted design studio known for its elegance, function, and attention to detail.



My mission is simple: to design spaces that not only look beautiful but also feel like home.
With a deep belief in the power of design to influence mood, productivity, and well-being, I lead a talented team in creating interiors that reflect our clients' values and vision.



From residential homes to boutique office spaces, our work is rooted in creativity,
clear communication, and a commitment to excellence. Projects like the Sunset Villas Makeover and Green Office Hub
have helped us build a reputation for client satisfaction and timeless design.



As a leader, I value integrity, consistency, and collaboration. I believe that interior design
is more than decoration it's storytelling through space.
I'm always excited to work with clients who want to bring new energy into their spaces. Let's design something memorable together.
